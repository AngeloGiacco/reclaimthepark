# Reclaim The Park

**A grassroots campaign to transform the Bayswater Road car park into something extraordinary for London.**

A ~1.25 hectare surface car park sits on Crown land at the north-western edge of Hyde Park — one of the most valuable and historically significant pieces of public land in Europe. It generates an estimated £0.5–1.5m/year in parking fees on land worth £93m+ per hectare. We believe London deserves better.

## What We Want

We don’t prescribe what should replace the car park. We want:

1. **Transparency** — public disclosure of the concession agreement between The Royal Parks and Euro Car Parks
1. **Vision** — an open design competition to imagine what this site could become
1. **Action** — a pilot activation to prove the site’s potential before the concession renews

## The Economic Case

|Current use                |Potential use                                         |
|---------------------------|------------------------------------------------------|
|~£1m/year car park revenue |£18–70m/year GVA (modelled on comparable London sites)|
|~5 jobs                    |150–400 direct jobs depending on concept              |
|150k vehicle movements/year|0.8–2m visitors/year                                  |

Over 10 years, inaction costs London an estimated **£170–510m in lost economic value**.
