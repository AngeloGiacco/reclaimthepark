# Councillor / Authority Letter Template

Use this template when writing to elected representatives or public authorities.

---

**To:** [Name and title]
**From:** [Your name]
**Date:** [Date]
**Subject:** [Brief, specific subject line]

---

Dear [Councillor / Minister / Name],

I am writing as a [resident of / visitor to / concerned citizen about] [area] regarding [specific issue].

**The issue:**
[One paragraph explaining the problem clearly and factually.]

**What we're asking for:**
[Specific, actionable request — e.g., "a public consultation on...", "release of data on...", "support for a pilot programme to..."]

**Why it matters:**
[Brief supporting evidence — reference data, comparables, or public interest.]

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss this further. Please let me know if you would be willing to meet or respond in writing.

Thank you for your time.

Yours sincerely,
[Your name]
[Your address, if writing to a ward councillor]
