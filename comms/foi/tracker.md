## Escalation Procedures

If a request is refused or partially refused:
1. Request internal review (free, must be done within 40 working days of refusal)
2. If internal review upheld, appeal to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)
3. Document all escalation steps in this tracker

## Notes

- The Royal Parks confirmed as a public authority for FOI purposes despite being a charity since 2017
- Environmental information may fall under the Environmental Information Regulations (EIR) 2004 — broader disclosure obligations than FOIA
