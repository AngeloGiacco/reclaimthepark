# Design Competitions

Open design ideas competitions run from this repo. These are not commissions — they are public provocations designed to generate compelling visions of what this site could become.

## Philosophy

* **Ideas, not solutions.** We seek bold visions that shift the Overton window, not buildable planning applications.
* **Open to everyone.** Architecture students, professional firms, hobbyists, children. The best idea can come from anywhere.
* **Agnostic on use.** Entries can propose anything: park extension, cultural venue, market, housing, art installation, re-wilding, or something nobody has thought of yet. The only requirement is that it's better than a car park.
* **Renders are the product.** The primary output is 3–5 beautiful images that we can use in the campaign. A compelling render does more political work than a 50-page report.

## Competition Lifecycle

1. Draft brief → reviewed by at least 2 people → published to `briefs/`
2. Open call → promoted via website, social media, architecture schools
3. Submission period → typically 8 weeks; entries go to `submissions/`
4. Judging → jury scores against published criteria in `judging/`
5. Results → winners announced, all entries exhibited publicly
6. Exhibition → ideally physical (in the park or at NLA) + permanent online gallery

## Partnerships

Target partnerships for competitions:

* **Architecture schools:** UCL Bartlett, AA School, RCA, Kingston, Westminster
* **Professional bodies:** RIBA, Landscape Institute
* **Media:** Dezeen, Architects' Journal, Evening Standard
* **Jury members:** Aim for 1 starchitect, 1 Royal Parks representative, 1 Westminster councillor, 1 landscape architect, 1 community voice
