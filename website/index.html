About the Campaign

London's Royal Parks are among the greatest public spaces in the world — but too much of their land is given over to roads, car parks, private concessions, and underused areas.

We're building the evidence base and public support to change that, through research, design competitions, and community organising.

Research

We use Freedom of Information requests, data analysis, and international comparisons to build an evidence-based case for better parks.

FOI Requests

Tracking how park land is used, who profits, and what the public gets in return.

Data Analysis

Mapping land use, footfall, revenue, and accessibility across all Royal Parks.

Comparables

Learning from King's Cross, the Serpentine, and great public spaces worldwide.

Design Competitions

We run open design competitions to crowdsource bold, practical ideas from architects, designers, and the public.
